Poly Path User/FAQ Page

Welcome to Poly Path! This tool can help you create customizable flowcharts for Cal Poly. You will be able to view the official catalog releases as an unregistered user, or if you have a Cal Poly log in, you can take full use of this site and create your own! If you have any questions, please refer to this page.

  • Log In and Out

  • How do I sign in?

    From the log in page, there should be a green log in button at the top-right hand corner of the page. Click this and a pop up window will direct you to enter your Cal Poly username and password. You can exit this box by finishing the process or by clicking anywhere outside the pop up window.

  • How do I sign out?

    From the log-in & flowchart page, click on your name in the top-right corner of the page. Click this and you will see the option to sign off.

  • How do I change my username/password?

    If you contact Cal Poly ITS they can help you change your Cal Poly username/password and this webservice should work. Click the "forgot password" in the log in pop up to be redirected to their page.

  • Search for Flowcharts

  • How do I search for a catalog flowchart?

    There are two ways to search for a flowchart. There are selection boxes to look for a flowchart by a major and catalog year on the log in page and in the opened flowchart page (top left corner).

  • Edit a Flowchart (For Registered Users)

  • How do I add a flowchart?

    To create a flowchart to your collection, first make sure the flowchart is open. Next, click the "save" button at the bottom of the page. If it is the first time the flowchart is saved, you will be prompted to name it.

  • How do I add a class?

    You can add a course by clicking a flowchart column (one of the quarters) and a pop up window will ask you for either the course name. There is also an option to look it up by the course's subject. Select the correct class and click "add" at the bottom of the page. The class will be added to that quarter.

  • How do I edit a class?

    You can edit a course by clicking the pencil icon at the top-right of the chosen class. A pop up window similar to the "adding a course" process will appear and ask you to find the course you wish to replace the current one. Click the "update" buttom at the bottom of the page and the class will be changed.

  • How do I remove a class?

    You can remove a course at any time by dragging the class box to the trash bin icon at the bottom right of the flowchart page.

  • How do I save a flowchart?

    On the flowchart page, you can click the save button to save a flowchart. This only will work if you have under the max amount of allowed flowcharts
